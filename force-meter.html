Force meter

Unveiling the Force Meter, a sport equipment device designed to elevate athletes' swimming performance. This tool accurately measures the pulling force exerted during swimming, providing invaluable insights for precision training. Tailored for swimmers, the Force Meter enhances training regimens by offering real-time feedback on exerted forces. Athletes can now optimize their technique and strength, pushing boundaries and achieving peak performance in the water with this device.
