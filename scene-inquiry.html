Scene investigation

Our Scene investigation project captures data with a "Spot" robot dog equipped with a 360 camera. This advanced technology captures comprehensive data for remote evaluation. Utilizing VR headsets, users can seamlessly immerse themselves in the scene, making informed decisions and assessments. The combination of robotic capability and immersive visualization enhances the efficiency and accuracy of investigations, redefining the landscape of scene analysis for various applications.
